About

Mboga Mtaani is an online grocery store that specializes in providing fresh vegetables and fruits to customers within Nairobi and its environs. We are committed to providing our customers with the freshest and healthiest produce at affordable prices. Our aim is to make it easy and convenient for people to access healthy and nutritious food, while also supporting local farmers and communities. Additionally, we are constantly looking for ways to improve our service and increase Mama Mboga's sales, so that we can reach even more customers and make a bigger impact in the lives of people and communities."

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, fresh produce at affordable prices while supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture practice

Our Vision

To be the leading online grocery store in Kenya, known for providing fresh and healthy produce while empowering customers to make healthy food choices and improve their lives.
